Strategy for Brock:
Brock uses Rock/Ground types (Geodude, Onix).
HYDRO (Squirtle Lv 10) is our main attacker with Bubble (Water-type, 4x super effective).
Bubble should 1HKO or 2HKO his Pokemon.
We have 1 Potion. If HYDRO's HP drops below 10, heal him immediately.
If HYDRO faints, the battle is likely lost, but we can try to use AERO's Sand-Attack to buy time, or FANG's Tail Whip + Tackle.
Rattata, Pidgey, Caterpie, Metapod will deal very little damage.
