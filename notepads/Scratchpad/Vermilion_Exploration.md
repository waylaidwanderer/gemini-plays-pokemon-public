- Fan Club Chairman Prompt Solution: The reason all scripts failed is that pressing A to clear the final text box's ▼ opens the prompt, but leaves the cursor on the default NO. A subsequent A press selects NO. The foolproof solution is to send "Up" before every "A" press. In normal text, "Up" is ignored. When the prompt opens, "Up" moves the cursor to "YES". The subsequent "A" then safely selects YES.
