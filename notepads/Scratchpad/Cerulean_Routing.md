Cerulean City Routing (Definitive):
- Exhaustive testing confirms there is NO walkable path North from the Southern half of Cerulean City (Y > 17) to the Northern half.
- The ledges at Y=15/17 are strictly one-way (Southbound).
- Route 9 is accessed via the East path blocked by the Team Rocket Grunt at (30, 8), which is in the Northern half.
- Macroscopic Loop Strategy: To return to the Northern half of Cerulean City and access Route 9, we MUST go South via the Cut tree at (19, 28) to Route 5 -> Route 6 -> Vermilion City -> Diglett's Cave -> Route 2 -> Pewter City -> Route 3 -> Mt. Moon -> Route 4 -> Cerulean City North.
- This route is optimal because we need to visit Route 2 via Diglett's Cave to obtain HM05 Flash from Oak's Aide anyway.
