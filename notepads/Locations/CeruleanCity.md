# Cerulean City
- Arrived Turn 2126
- Connected to Route 4 (West, one-way ledges prevent returning to Mt. Moon)
- Trade House: (13, 15)
- Pokemon Center: (19, 17)
- Robbed House: (9, 11)
- Poke Mart: (25, 25)
[City Layout & Traversal]
- The city is divided horizontally by one-way ledges (y=15) that only allow southbound travel (jumping down).
- You cannot walk directly north from the main southern street to Route 24.
- To reach Route 24 from the Pokemon Center (19, 17), walk left to x=8, then walk north through the alleyway, then right to the bridge at x=10.
