- Connects Pewter City (West) to Mt. Moon (East).
- Entrance from Pewter City is around X=40, Y=18.

- Bug Catcher at (24, 6) facing Right.
- Youngster at (22, 9) facing Up.
- NPC (Blue sprite, likely a Lass) at (33, 10) facing Down.
