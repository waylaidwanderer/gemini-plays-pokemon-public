Route 3 Layout:
- Connects Pewter City (West) to Mt. Moon (East).
- Entrance from Pewter City is around X=40, Y=18.
- The initial area from Pewter City is plain ground, not tall grass. Must continue East to find encounters.
- Route 3 requires navigating around several one-way ledges (Obstacle/Ledge_OneWayDown). A ledge blocks Northward movement at Y=7.
