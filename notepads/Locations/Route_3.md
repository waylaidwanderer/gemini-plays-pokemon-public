Route 3 Layout:
- Connects Pewter City (West) to Mt. Moon (East).
- Entrance from Pewter City is around X=40, Y=18.
- The initial area from Pewter City is plain ground, not tall grass. Must continue East to find encounters.
- Route 3 requires navigating around several one-way ledges (Obstacle/Ledge_OneWayDown). A ledge blocks Northward movement at Y=7.
- The tall grass area is bounded by trees to the West (X=23) and ledges to the South (Y=7).
- To return West to Pewter City from the grass, head East to the gap in the Y=7 ledge at X=27 to move North/South and bypass the ledges.
Trainers:
- Lass at (23, 4) facing Down.
- Bug Catcher at (24, 6) facing Right.
- Youngster at (22, 9) facing Up.
- NPC (Blue sprite, likely a Lass) at (33, 10) facing Down.
