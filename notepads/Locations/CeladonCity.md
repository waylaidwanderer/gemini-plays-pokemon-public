# Celadon City

[Points of Interest]
- Pokemon Center: East side of the city (arriving from Route 7).
- The back alley behind Celadon Mansion is a trap. X=5 is a solid wall from Y=2 to Y=12, and X=8 is also a wall. The only way out of this specific alley is back north to Route 16.
- Map 0_130 is the Game Freak HQ (Celadon Mansion 3F), accessible via the back entrance. It is NOT Route 16.
