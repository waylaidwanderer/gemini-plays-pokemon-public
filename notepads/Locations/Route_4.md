Route 4 Layout (East of Mt. Moon):
- Pokémon Center located at (11, 5).
- Path West of the PC terminates at a solid rock wall at X=3 and Y=3. This is a dead end.
- Mt. Moon cave entrance is located at (18, 5), East of the Pokémon Center. Signpost at (17, 7).
- Route 4 (Post Mt. Moon): Exit is at (18, 5). Going South leads to one-way ledges at Y=9 and Y=13.
- TRAP WARNING: Jumping South over the ledges at (17, 9) and (17, 13) traps you in a lower corridor (Y=14/Y=15). This corridor is blocked to the East by a Mountain Wall at X=20 and bounded South by a cliff at Y=16. The only open path is West. Do NOT jump these ledges if attempting to travel East to Cerulean City!
