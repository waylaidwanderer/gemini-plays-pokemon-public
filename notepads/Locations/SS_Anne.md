S.S. Anne Layout:
- Entrance is at the North (X=14, Y=0).
- South of the entrance are stairs at (14, 1)/(14, 2) leading further into the ship.
- Taking the stairs at the entrance (14, 2) warps to a narrow North-South hallway starting at (27, 0). This hallway is a dead end, blocked by a stationary Sailor at (27, 5). Must explore the main entrance floor laterally.
