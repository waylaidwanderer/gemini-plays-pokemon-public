S.S. Anne Layout:
- Main entrance gangway (14,0)-(14,2) is a 1-tile wide vertical path.
- Entrance stairs at (14,2) warp to a large vertical hallway at (27,0).
- This vertical hallway is 2-tiles wide (X=26 and X=27).
- Sailor at (27,5) blocks the East side, but the West side (X=26) is open.
- The vertical hallway meets a horizontal hallway at Y=6 and Y=7.
- There are cabin doors marked by blue circles, for example at (22,5) and (30,5).
- Further paths go South at X=23 and X=31 at Y=8.
