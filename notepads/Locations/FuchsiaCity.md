# Fuchsia City
- Fishing Guru's Brother (Good Rod): (27, 27)
- Main city accessed via Cut tree at (26, 13).
- PokeMart: (5, 13) - Sells: Ultra Ball, Great Ball, Super Potion...
- Pokemon Center: (19, 27)
- Path to Pokemon Center: From the West side (X=1), walk South to Y=32. Walk East to X=8, North through the gap to Y=30, East to X=19, then North to enter the Center at (19, 27). DO NOT approach from the East side, as it is blocked by one-way ledges.
- To return North from the Pokemon Center: Walk East, jump RIGHT over the ledge at (23, 30) to X=24, walk North, and use Cut on the bush at (26, 13).
- Safari Zone Warden's House: (11, 27). He lost his Gold Teeth in the Safari Zone!
