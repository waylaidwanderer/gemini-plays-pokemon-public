# Rocket Hideout B1F
[Start: Turn 10026]
- The TRUE ELEVATOR PATH is from the B2F spinner maze.
- Take the stairs from B2F to arrive at B1F (21, 24) (South section).
- From (21, 24), walk along X=22 and find the gap in the wall at X=23.
- Once through the gap, walk to the elevator pad at (24, 19).
- The North section (Game Corner Entrance) is a dead end for reaching the elevator due to a wall at Y=16.
