# Route 15
- Connected to Route 14 on the east.
- Heading west towards Fuchsia City.
[Navigation: West to East (Lower Path)]
- The lower path of Route 15 has a solid bush wall around X=10. The bush at (11, 32) is a FAKE Cut bush (no sprite) and blocks the way.
- To bypass it, you must walk all the way South to Y=40, then head East.
- At X=17, a tree wall blocks heading further East. You must walk North along this tree wall.
- The gap to the upper path is found by navigating around the jutting trees to (11, 21) and walking straight North to a plateau at Y=13.
