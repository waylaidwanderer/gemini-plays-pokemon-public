# Route 5
- Located South of Cerulean City.
- Saffron City Gate: Located in the center lane (approx x=10). BLOCKED by thirsty guards.
- Underground Path: Located in the eastern lane at (17, 27).
- To reach it: Go through the Robbed House in Cerulean City at (27, 11), exit its back hole, and walk south to enter Route 5 at x=17 or x=18. Walk straight down the x=17 lane to the Underground Path entrance.
- CRITICAL: I previously hallucinated that (17, 27) was the Saffron Gate. It is NOT. (17, 27) IS the Underground Path.
