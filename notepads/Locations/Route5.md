# Route 5
- Located South of Cerulean City.
- Saffron City Gate: Center lane (approx x=10, y=27). BLOCKED by thirsty guards.
- Underground Path: Far-right lane. Door is at (17, 27).
- To reach Underground Path: Exit Cerulean City at x=28 to enter Route 5 at x=18. Walk straight south to the building, and the door is one step left at (17, 27).
