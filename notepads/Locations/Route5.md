# Route 5
- Located South of Cerulean City.
- Saffron City Gate: (17, 27). BLOCKED by thirsty guards.
- Underground Path: To reach it, DO NOT enter Route 5 from the main south exit of Cerulean City. Go through the Robbed House in Cerulean City at (27, 11), exit its back hole, and walk south along the FAR east edge of Cerulean City (ideally x=34). This aligns you with the correct far-right lane of Route 5 (x=24), bypassing the trapped ledges. Exiting Cerulean at x=28 puts you at Route 5 x=18, which is the wrong lane and leads to the blocked Saffron Gate.
