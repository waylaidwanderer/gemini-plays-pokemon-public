# Game Mechanics & Systems
## Tile Mechanics (Verified)
- FLOOR / TALL_GRASS: Walkable.
- WALL / COUNTER / FLOOR_UP_WALL (from North): Impassable.
- LEDGE_HOP_DOWN: One-way North to South. (Verified at Row 21, 23, 34).
- Blackthorn Elevation: Eastern half is tiered. Row 21 ledges block northward passage for x > 4, with potential gaps at x=9, 19, 33.

## Type Effectiveness
- Ghost (Xenon) immune to Normal (Selfdestruct). Lead Xenon for Graveler encounters.
