## How to Solve Boulder Puzzle
- Goal: Push three boulders into pits to create a path on 1F.
- Pits: P1 (8, 3), P2 (2, 5), P3 (8, 7).
- Boulders: B6 (3, 2), B7 (6, 1), B8 (8, 13).
- Strategy:
  1. Push B6 (3, 2) into P2 (2, 5).
  2. Push B7 (6, 1) into P1 (8, 3).
  3. Push B8 (8, 13) into P3 (8, 7).

## Tile Mechanics (2F)
- FLOOR: Standard traversable tile.
- WALL: Impassable barrier.
- PIT: Target for boulders; player falls to 1F.
- LADDER: Warp to 1F; resets boulder positions.
- NPC: Treated as WALL.
