# Tile Mechanics
- FLOOR_UP_WALL: Blocks SOUTH movement. Allows NORTH movement.
- LEDGE_HOP_DOWN: Blocks NORTH movement.
- BUOY: Water wall. Gaps at (19, 21), (23, 15), (27, 9).

# Suicune Quest (Cianwood)
## Strategy: Northern Sea Landing
Reach the northern plateau (14, 10) by Surfing north through the buoy gap at (19, 21) and landing on the northern shore near X=16.

## Execution Plan
1. Move to (23, 32) and Surf into the Outer Sea.
2. Navigate to (19, 21) and pass through the buoy gap.
3. Navigate to Row 11-12 and attempt to land at (16, 11) or (16, 12).
4. Walk to (14, 10) to trigger Suicune sighting.
