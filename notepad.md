## Suicune Capture Strategy (Static Encounter)
- **Lead:** XENON (Gastly) Lv21.
- **Moves:** Hypnosis (sleep), Night Shade (fixed damage).
- **Items:** 43 Great Balls, 1 Ultra Ball.
- **Note:** Suicune is a static encounter in Tin Tower and does NOT flee. Mean Look is not required. Hypnosis is priority for easier catch.
