# Persistence Knowledge
## Tile Mechanics (Global)
- FLOOR: Traversable.
- WALL: Impassable.
- LADDER: Floor change.
- WARP_CARPET_DOWN: Map transition.
- COUNTER: Face and press A to interact.
- WATER: Requires SURF.
- LEDGE: Semi-traversable (jump down).
- GRASS: Wild encounter zone.
- HOLE: Fall to floor below.
