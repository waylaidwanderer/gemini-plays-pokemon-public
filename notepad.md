# Suicune Quest Strategy: 'The West Corridor'
- Goal: Reach Suicune sighting area at (7, 4).
- Problem: The city is split by vertical walls. Water is blocked by buoys.
- Solution: Use the crossing at Row 33/34 and the corridor at X=2.
- Route:
    1. Walk west along Row 33 to (6, 33).
    2. Move to (6, 34) -> (2, 34) to bypass the X=5 cliff.
    3. Walk north along X=2 to (2, 14).
    4. Walk east to (7, 14), then north to (7, 4).
- Battle Strategy: Lead with XENON (Haunter) for Hypnosis. Be ready for Eusine.
- Status: Talking to Pokefan M, then moving to (6, 33).
