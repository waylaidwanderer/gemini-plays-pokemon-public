# Suicune Quest (Cianwood)

1. Descend X=12 to Row 44 (Gap at 12, 34).
2. Cross X=9 gap at (9, 44) to X=6 corridor.
3. Ascend X=6 to Row 35 (Crosses Row 46, 48 ledges; Row 34 blocks South).

5. Ascend X=2 to Row 12 (Bypasses Row 15 ledge).
6. Cross Row 12 to (14, 12) and reach (14, 10).
