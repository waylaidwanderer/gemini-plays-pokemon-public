## Strategy: The Great Southern Detour to Suicune
Use the 'Great Southern Detour' (Row 53 and X=2) to reach the northern plateau, bypassing all central wall blockades.

### Verified Land Route
1. (9, 44) -> (19, 44) -> (19, 49) -> (19, 53): Walk East and South to the southern map edge.
2. (19, 53) -> (10, 53) -> (10, 51) -> (2, 51): Walk West to the West Beach Highway.
3. (2, 51) -> (2, 14): Walk North along the beach highway to the plateau base.
4. (2, 14) -> (8, 14): Walk East to the plateau entrance.
5. (8, 14) -> (8, 10) -> (14, 10): Walk North and East to the Suicune trigger point.

## Global Tile Mechanics (Cianwood)
- FLOOR: Traversable.
- WALL: Impassable.
- WATER: Requires Surf.
- BUOY: Impassable hard wall.
- LEDGE_HOP_DOWN: One-way jump (North to South).
- FLOOR_UP_WALL: Impassable cliff face.

## Battle Strategy: Eusine
- Lead: Calcifer (Lv 64 Typhlosion).
- Strategy: Sweep with Flamethrower. OHKO potential on most targets.

## Navigation Notes
- Row 44 Passage: Clear horizontal path from X=9 to X=19.
- X=19 Corridor: Clear vertical path from Row 44 to Row 53.
- Row 53 Highway: Clear horizontal path from X=22 down to X=8.
- West Beach Highway: X=0-2 is a clear vertical path from Row 53 to Row 14.
- Northern Plateau Entry: Only accessible from Row 14 via the X=8 vertical corridor.
- Gym Blockade: Building at (10, 40) and associated walls block all central land access to the north.
