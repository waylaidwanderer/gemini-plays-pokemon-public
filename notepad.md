# Team Rocket HQ (Mahogany Town)
## Map IDs
- **3_51**: B1F (Main Floor).
- **3_50**: B2F (Basement).

## Primary Objectives
1. **Herd Murkrow**: Guide the Murkrow from B1F (7, 2) to the Boss Door.
2. **Open Boss Door**: Use "RATICATE TAIL" + Murkrow's "HAIL GIOVANNI" at B2F (23, 14).
3. **Defeat Executive**: Inside the Boss Room.

## Murkrow Status
- **Location**: B1F (7, 2).
- **Knowledge**: Teaches "HAIL GIOVANNI".
- **Mechanic**: Fleeing AI. Must chase it to specific spots.
- **Route Hypothesis**: B1F (7, 2) -> ??? -> Boss Door.

## Key Discoveries & Mechanics
- **Passwords**: "RATICATE TAIL" (Grunt), "HAIL GIOVANNI" (Murkrow).
- **Switch #1**: B1F (19, 11). Disables statues. Found.
- **Secret Passage**: B1F (19, 10). Fake computer terminal.
- **Warp Trap**: B1F (26, 9). Disabled.
- **Fake Machines**: TYPE_3fe2 machines are walkable (e.g. 19, 10).
- **Locked Shutters**: B1F (14/15, 11).
- **Locked Gate**: B2F (3, 8).

## Navigation
- **B1F <-> B2F Stairs**:
  - NW: (3, 6) <-> (3, 6)
  - NE: (27, 2) <-> (27, 2)
  - SE: (27, 14) <-> (27, 14)
- **Obstacles**: Grunt at B2F (4, 1) can be bypassed by walking South of him at (4, 2). B2F North Corridor is navigable.
