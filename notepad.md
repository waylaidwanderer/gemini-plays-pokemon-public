# Tile Mechanics
- FLOOR_UP_WALL: Blocks SOUTH movement. Allows NORTH movement. Found at Row 34 (X=6-8), Row 46, Row 48, Row 50.
- LEDGE_HOP_DOWN: Blocks NORTH movement. Found at Row 15.
- Vertical Walls: X=3, 5, 9, 11, 17.

# Suicune Quest (Cianwood)
## Strategy: Western Corridor Land Route
Reach (14, 10) by using the Western Corridor (X=0-2) to bypass the Row 15 ledge.
1. Land at (23, 32) and move West along Row 32 to (12, 32).
2. Move to (12, 33) and West to (6, 33) via gap at (9, 33).
3. Move to (6, 35) and West to (2, 35) via gap at (5, 35).
4. Move North along X=2 to (2, 12).
5. Move East to (14, 12) and North to (14, 10).
