# Tile Mechanics
- FLOOR_UP_WALL: Blocks SOUTH movement. Allows NORTH movement. Found at Row 34, 46, 48, 50.
- LEDGE_HOP_DOWN: Blocks NORTH movement. Found at (10, 15).
- Vertical Walls: Nested walls at X=3, 5, 9, 11. Gaps exist at specific rows.

# Suicune Quest (Cianwood)
## Strategy: Western Corridor Land Route
Reach the northern plateau (14, 10) by weaving through wall gaps to the Western Corridor (X=2). This bypasses the Row 15 land ledge.
1. Land at (23, 32).
2. Move West to (6, 33) using gaps at (9, 33). (Bypass Pokefan M at (16, 33) via Row 32).
3. Move West to (2, 35) using gaps at (5, 35) and (3, 35).
4. Ascend Western Corridor (X=2) to Row 12.
5. Move East to (14, 12) and North to (14, 10).
