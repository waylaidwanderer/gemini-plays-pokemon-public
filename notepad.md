# Tile Mechanics (Global)
- FLOOR: Standard ground. Traversable.
- WATER: Surf required. Traversable.
- BUOY: Impassable water obstacle.
- WALL: Impassable ground/object.
- FLOOR_UP_WALL: Impassable ledge face. Acts as a solid wall from all directions.
- LEDGE_HOP_DOWN: One-way jump (North to South).

# Strategy: Capture Suicune (The Strip 1 West Beach Highway)
- Start Turn: 48452
- Step 1: Reach Strip 3 (X=10) via gap at (9, 33).
- Step 2: Reach Strip 2 South (X=8) via gap at (9, 49).
- Step 3: Reach Strip 1 (X=2) via gap at (5, 48).
- Step 4: Walk North along West Beach (Strip 1) to Row 10.
- Step 5: Walk East along Row 10 corridor to Suicune sighting spot (14, 10).
- Verified: Row 10 is a horizontal bridge across the top of the map. Strip 1 is the only continuous north-south path.
