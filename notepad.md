# Team Rocket HQ (Mahogany Town)
## Objectives
- **Primary**: Open Boss Door on B2F (Requires Murkrow Voice).
- **Secondary**: Find Murkrow (Real) or Switch to materialize/catch it.
- **Tertiary**: Disable Persian Statues (B1F).

## Status
- **Location**: B2F Central Area.
- **Silver**: B2F (21, 14). Dialogue: "Only the boss knows the password."
- **Lance**: B2F (20, 13). Appears to be a non-solid hologram/ghost.
- **Boss Door**: B2F (23, 14). Locked. Needs password + Murkrow.
- **Passwords**: "Hail Giovanni", "SLOWPOKETAIL".

## Key Mechanics
- **Persian Statues (B1F)**: Alarms. Need switch.
- **Shutters (B1F)**: Blocking central access.
- **Murkrow**: Found as ghost on B1F and B2F? Need to find the real one.

## Exploration Plan
1. Traverse B2F East Wing from SE (27, 14) to NE (27, 2).
2. Ascend to B1F (27, 2).
3. Walk West on B1F to Secret Corridor (Row 1).
4. Take Secret Corridor to NW Stairs (3, 2).
5. Descend to B2F (3, 2) (West Wing).
6. Find Murkrow at (7, 1).

## Important Coordinates
- **Scientist Mitch**: B1F (11, 15)
- **Stairs**: B2F (27, 2) [NE], (3, 6) [SW], (27, 14) [SE].
- **West Wing Structure**: Confirmed B2F West side is split by a wall at Row 4. SW area (accessed via stairs at 3,6) is isolated from NW area (accessed via stairs at 3,2). Must use B1F to transit between them.
