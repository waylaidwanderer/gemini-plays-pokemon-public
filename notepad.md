# Goldenrod Underground Switch Puzzle

- **WALL/Shutter (Closed):** Impassable.
- **Shutter (Open):** Walkable.
- **Switch:** Toggles shutter states.
- **LADDER/DOOR/WARP:** Transitions to other areas.
