## Murkrow Puzzle (B2F)
- **Status**: Player (10, 4), Murkrow (10, 3).
- **Strategy**: "The Double Block Desync".
  1. Move Down to (10, 5). P(10, 5), M(10, 4).
  2. Move Right to (11, 5).
     - Murkrow blocked by Desk at (11, 4).
     - Result: P(11, 5), M(10, 4).
  3. Move Down x3 to (11, 8). P(11, 8), M(10, 7).
  4. Move Right x2 to (13, 8). P(13, 8), M(12, 7).
  5. Move Up x2.
     - P blocked by Plant at (13, 7).
     - M moves Up to (12, 5).
     - Result: P(13, 8), M(12, 5).
  6. Slide Right to Victory.
- **Goal**: Lead Murkrow to Boss Door at (23, 14).
