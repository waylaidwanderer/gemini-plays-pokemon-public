# Strategy: Trigger Suicune Sighting
- Plan: The Up-Wall Shortcut (Fastest).
- Steps:
  1. Walk East to (6, 19).
  2. Walk South to (6, 24).
  3. Walk West to (4, 24).
  4. Walk North to (4, 21).
  5. Move Up to climb slope at (4, 20).
  6. Walk West to (2, 20).
  7. Walk North along Column 2 to (2, 14).
  8. Walk East along Row 14 to (8, 14).
  9. Walk North through the gap at (8, 13) to (8, 12).
  10. Walk East along Row 12 to (14, 12).
  11. Walk North to (14, 10).
- Battle Prep: Lead Calcifer (Lv64 Typhlosion).

# Tile Mechanics
- FLOOR: Standard traversable tile.
- WALL / BUOY: Impassable collision.
- WATER: Requires SURF.
- LEDGE_HOP_DOWN: One-way jump (South). Blocks North.
- FLOOR_UP_WALL: One-way climb slope. Approach from South (Up move) to climb. Impassable from North (Acts as WALL).
- BREAKABLE ROCK: Requires ROCK SMASH.
