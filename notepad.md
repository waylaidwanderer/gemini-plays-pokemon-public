# Suicune Quest: Cianwood City
## Strategy: Western Highway via Row 46
The northern plateau (14, 10) is accessible via the western coast corridor (X=2).
1. Navigate to (2, 46) via Row 46 (gap at X=6). (In Progress)
2. Walk north along X=2 to Row 10.
3. Walk east to trigger Suicune at (14, 10).

## Tile Mechanics
- FLOOR: Traversable.
- WALL, BUOY, DOOR: Impassable.
- WATER: Requires Surf.
- FLOOR_UP_WALL: Blocks DOWN movement (e.g., Row 50, Row 46 at X=21-28, Row 34).
- LEDGE_HOP_DOWN: Blocks UP movement (e.g., (10, 15)).
