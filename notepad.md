## Battle Strategy
- **Target:** Suicune (Lv 40, Base Speed 85).
- **Lead:** KIMCHI (Lv 21, Base Speed 40).
- **Mechanics:** Suicune flees on its first turn. Since KIMCHI is slower, it will flee before Sleep Powder.
- **Goal:** Chip damage and status (persistent).
- **Catch Rate:** ~0.39% (Full HP, No Status, Great Ball).
- **Contingency:** If progress stalls, catch a faster Pokémon with Mean Look or Hypnosis (e.g., Haunter).
