## Training Strategy
- Switch Training: Lead with a strong Pokémon (e.g., Gneiss or Calcifer) and immediately switch to a lower-level Pokémon (e.g., Rocky, Egg, Apophis) on Turn 1 of a battle. This splits EXP and ensures safety.
