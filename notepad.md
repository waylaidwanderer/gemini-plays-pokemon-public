# Suicune Quest (Cianwood)
## Strategy: Outer Sea Southern Loop (Row 51)
Reach the northern plateau (14, 10) by bypassing all land-based ledges via the Outer Sea.
1. Move to (23, 32) and Surf.
2. Swim South to Row 51 and land at (23, 51).
3. Walk West along Row 51 to the Western Corridor (X=2).
4. Walk North along X=2 to Row 12 (bypasses Row 15 ledge).
5. Walk East along Row 12 to (14, 12) and North to (14, 10).
