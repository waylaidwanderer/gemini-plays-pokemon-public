# Suicune Quest: Cianwood City
## Active Strategy: The Row 33/35 Crossing
The northern plateau (14, 10) is accessible via the Western Corridor (X=0-2). This corridor can be reached by crossing the city's internal walls using a series of gaps at Row 33 and Row 35.

### The Path to Suicune
1. Navigate to (6, 33) via the clear Row 33 corridor. (In Progress)
2. Walk South to (6, 35) to bypass the wall at X=5.
3. Walk West to (4, 35) to enter the western city clearway.
4. Walk South to Row 51 at (4, 51).
5. Walk West to (2, 51) to enter the Western Corridor.
6. Walk North along X=2 to Row 12 at (2, 12).
7. Walk East along Row 12 to (14, 12).
8. Walk North to trigger Suicune at (14, 10).
