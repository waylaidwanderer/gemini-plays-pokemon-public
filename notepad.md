1. Move to (12, 44) via X=12 gap at (12, 34).
2. Move West to (6, 44) via gap at (9, 44).
3. Move North to (6, 35) and West to (2, 35) via gaps at (5, 35) and (3, 35).

5. Walk East to (14, 12) and North to (14, 10).
