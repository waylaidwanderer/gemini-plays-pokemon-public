# Suicune Quest (Cianwood)
## Strategy: Outer Sea Surf Bypass
Reach the northern plateau (14, 10) by Surfing in the Outer Sea (X=24). This bypasses the land-based wall/ledge maze.
1. Surf at (23, 32).
2. Pass through buoy gap at (23, 15).
3. Land at northern shore near (16, 11).
4. Walk to (14, 10) to trigger Suicune.
