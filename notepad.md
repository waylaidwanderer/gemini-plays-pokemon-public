# Suicune Quest (Cianwood)
## Strategy: Great Southern Loop (Row 51)
Reach the northern plateau (14, 10) via the Western Corridor (X=0-2). Access to this corridor is obtained at Row 51. To reach Row 51 without being blocked by ledges, Gem must use the eastern thoroughfare (X=24).

## Execution Plan
1. Move to (24, 44) via Row 44 (bypasses Gym, PokeCenter, and X=17 wall).
2. Move to (24, 51) (bypasses Row 46, 48, 50 ledges).
3. Move West to (2, 51).
4. Move North along Western Corridor (X=2) to Row 12.
5. Walk East to (14, 12) and North to (14, 10).
