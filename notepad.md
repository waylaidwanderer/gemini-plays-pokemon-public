## Murkrow Puzzle (B2F)
- **Status**: Player (7, 5), Murkrow (7, 4).
- **Strategy**: "The West Wall Offset".
  1. Setup: P(7, 5), M(7, 4).
  2. Move Right to (10, 5). P(10, 5), M(10, 4).
  3. Move Down to (10, 10). P(10, 10), M(10, 9).
  4. **THE SHIFT**: Move Left to (6, 10).
     - P passes through Gap at (6, 10).
     - M blocked by Wall at (6, 9).
     - Result: P(6, 10), M(7, 9). (M is Right of P).
  5. Move Right to (13, 10). P(13, 10), M(14, 9).
  6. **THE DROP**: Move Down.
     - P blocked by (13, 11).
     - M moves to (14, 10).
  7. Result: M is at Shutter (14, 10).
- **Goal**: Lead Murkrow to Boss Door at (23, 14).

## Key Info
- **Boss Door**: Needs Voice ID (Murkrow).

## Map Notes
- **Walls**: (6, 9) is the key blocking tile for the Shift. (13, 11) is the key block for the Drop.
- **Crossing**: (6, 10) is the Gap.
- **Traps**: Warp Trap at (26, 10).
