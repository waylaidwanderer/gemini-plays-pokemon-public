# Suicune Quest: Cianwood City
## Objective
Trigger the Suicune sighting at (14, 10) in Cianwood City (Map 22_3).

## Strategy
1. Enter Cianwood City (Map 22_3) via the Route 41 (0, 45) warp.
2. Navigate to the northern plateau by Surfing within the city or finding the internal land path.
3. Suicune is at (14, 10). Eusine will challenge us afterward.

## Tile Mechanics
- FLOOR: Land (Traversable).
- WATER: Water (Requires Surf).
- FLOOR_UP_WALL: One-way North slopes (Ascent only).
- LEDGE_HOP_DOWN: One-way South ledges (Descent only).
- Route 41 Buoys: Column 5 and Row 50-51 form a barrier. Column 0-1 pocket at Row 51 is unreachable from the east.

## Key Coordinates (Map 22_3)
- (29, 45): Entry from Route 41.
- (12, 28): Surf point to central lake.
- (13, 16): Landing point for northern plateau.
- (14, 10): Suicune trigger point.
