# Tile Mechanics
- FLOOR_UP_WALL: Blocks SOUTH movement. Allows NORTH movement. Found at Row 34, 46, 48, 50.
- LEDGE_HOP_DOWN: Blocks NORTH movement. Found at (10, 15).
- Vertical Walls: X=3, 5, 9, 11.

# Suicune Quest (Cianwood)
## Strategy: Staggered Ledge Descent (Row 51)
Reach the northern plateau (14, 10) via the Western Corridor (X=0-2). Access to X=2 is obtained at Row 51. Gem must use staggered gaps to move south through one-way ledges.
1. Move to (12, 34) and pass through gap to Row 45.
2. Move to (18, 46) and pass through gap to Row 47.
3. Move to (12, 48) and pass through gap to Row 49.
4. Move to (10, 50) and pass through gap to Row 51.
5. Walk West to (2, 51) and North along Western Corridor (X=2) to Row 12.
6. Walk East along Row 12 to (14, 12) and North to (14, 10).
