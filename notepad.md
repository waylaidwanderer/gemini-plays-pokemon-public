# Warehouse Switch Puzzle - Strategy
- Trap: (12, 9) warps to (12, 5).
- Trap: (10, 1) appears to be a warp/trap. Attempting to interact with Switch 2 from (10, 2).
- Current Status: Switch 1 ON, Switch 3 ON, Switch 2 OFF.
- Shutter Status: (12, 8) Open, (6, 8) Closed.
- Goal: Turn Switch 2 ON, then check Shutter 2 (6, 8).

## Plan
1. Stand at (10, 2).
2. Face Up and Press A (Toggle Switch 2).
3. If successful, navigate to (6, 8).
4. Check if Shutter 2 is Open.
5. If Open, check for traps at (6, 9) and proceed to Director (9, 12).
