# Tile Mechanics
- FLOOR: Traversable. Standard ground.
- WALL: Impassable. Structural obstacles or boundary walls.
- MART_SHELF: Impassable. Display shelves inside shops.
- FLOOR_UP_WALL: Impassable. Vertical ledge faces that block movement (e.g., at (19, 16)).
- LEDGE_HOP_DOWN: One-way traversable. Can jump down from above to the tile below, but cannot be entered from the bottom or sides.
- PIT: Impassable. Causes falling to the floor below (e.g., in Blackthorn Gym).
- LADDER: Warp between floors (e.g., in Blackthorn Gym).
- BOULDER: Impassable unless pushed. Can be moved with Strength (HM04).
- LAVA: Impassable. Functions as WALL collision in Blackthorn Gym.
- WATER: Traversable only with Surf (HM03).
