# Game Mechanics
## Tile Mechanics
- FLOOR: Walkable. NOTE: B1F floor tiles do NOT slide. B2F floor tiles (non-islands) DO slide.
- WALL: Impassable.
- WATER: Needs Surf (HM03).
- ICE: Sliding mechanic. B1F northern corridor (Row 1-2) and B2F large rink.
- PIT: Warp to B2F. Filling a pit with a boulder creates a stopper on B2F.
- BOULDER: Pushable with Strength. Resets to start position when changing floors via ladder. Does NOT reset when falling through a pit.
- STRENGTH: Must be re-activated after every floor transition or battle. Verified.

## Battle Strategy
- Leader Clair: Dragonair Lv37 x3, Kingdra Lv40.
- Strategy: Typhlosion (Lv45) w/ Thunderpunch, Graveler (Lv44) w/ Earthquake.
