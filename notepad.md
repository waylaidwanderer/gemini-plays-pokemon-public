# Game Mechanics & Systems
## Tile Mechanics
- FLOOR: Traversable.
- WALL / BUOY: Impassable.
- WATER: Traversable via Surf.
- LEDGE_HOP_DOWN: One-way (South).
- FLOOR_UP_WALL (Terrace): One-way (North).
- ROCK (Object): Traversable after Rock Smash.
