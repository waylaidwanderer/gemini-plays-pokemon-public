# Suicune Quest: Cianwood City
## Active Strategy: The Great Sea Loop (Row 51)
The northern plateau (14, 10) is blocked by rocks and walls. The only land path is via the Western Corridor (X=0-2), which must be reached by Surfing around the southern city ledges.

### The Path to Suicune
1. Walk to the eastern shore at (27, 44). (In Progress)
2. Surf East and South into the ocean.
3. Navigate to Row 51 and land on the southern beach.
4. Walk West along Row 51 to the Western Corridor (X=2).
5. Walk North along X=2 to Row 12.
6. Walk East and North to trigger Suicune at (14, 10).
