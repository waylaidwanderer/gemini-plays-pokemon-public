# Tile Mechanics (Global)
- FLOOR: Passable. Standard movement.
- WALL: Impassable. (Includes 'SILVER BLOCKS')
- PIT: Player falls to 1F. Boulders create bridges on 1F.
- LADDER: Warp between floors. Resets boulder positions. Deactivates Strength.

# Blackthorn Gym 2F Puzzle Analysis
- Boulders (Initial): B6 (3, 3), B7 (6, 1), B8 (8, 14).
- Verified: B7 (6, 1), B8 (8, 14).
- Pits: P1 (2, 5), P2 (8, 3), P3 (8, 7).

# Definitive Strategy
1. Confirm B6 at (3, 3) and activate Strength.
2. Solve B7 (6, 1): Push Right to (8, 1), then Down to (8, 3) [PIT].
3. Solve B6 (3, 3): Push Up to (3, 1), Left to (1, 1), Down to (1, 6), Right to (2, 6), then Up to (2, 5) [PIT].
4. Solve B8 (8, 14): Push Up to (8, 11), move to (9, 11), push Left to (6, 11), Up to (6, 7), then Right to (8, 7) [PIT].
- Note: Row 1 is the only full-width crossing path. Column 6 is the main north-south artery.
